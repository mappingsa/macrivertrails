Food and accommodation for groups over 28 people, up to 160+. Great meals and comfy beds. Beautiful views during the day of the surrounding hills and Lake Burrendong, 900 feet below! Bush walks, play equipment, jumping pillow, climbing tower, oval and basket ball court. How to find us: Last driveway on the left before the wall of Burrendong dam. See our website for a map.
Are there teachers/experts/guides available? Yes, but you need to pre-book.
Is there educational interpretive signage present? Yes, themed displays including a rainforest, plant family groupings, bush tucker and waterwise gardens.

OPENING HOURS: Are flexible, but please call ahead.
