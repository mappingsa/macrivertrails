This is a quiet and peaceful rest stop beside a classic Australian waterhole with big old Red gums. There are picnic tables, toilets, kids play ground, bar-b-ques etc. Camping is not allowed. Access is sealed. There are garbage bins.

OPENING HOURS: Not applicable

COORDINATES: 32º1'53.42"S, 147º59'13.40"E

LOCATION: Driving from Warren the Park is on your left just after you cross the railways lines on the road to Narromine. From Narromine it's just after you cross the bridge coming into town on a sweeping right hand bend.
