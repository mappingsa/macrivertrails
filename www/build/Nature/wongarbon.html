This is a 30 hectare reserve on the edge of the township of Wongarbon which includes a path for walking through an area of grasslands and woodlands. There is also a large dam. The area offers good bird watching and in the spring, wildlflowers. No camping allowed. There are no facilities.

OPENING HOURS: Not applicable - no booking necessary.
