Lots of lake activities here - canoeing, kayaking and dragon boating for groups. Self contained accommodation for people to bring their own vessels.

OPENING HOURS: Prearranged visits only - please telephone first.

COORDINATES: 32º42'27.48"S, 149º 6'53.98"E

LOCATION: : Lake Burrendong Sport and Recreation is only one hour's drive from either Dubbo or Orange and five hours from Sydney. If you are coming from the north of the state, the park is 27km south-east of Wellington.
