The Old Dubbo Gaol was a courthouse lockup in 1847, was proclaimed an official Gaol in 1859. It closed operations as a penal institution in 1966. Officially re-opened as a tourist attraction in 1974, the Old Dubbo Gaol is now fully restored and is home to a large display of animatronic models and holographic displays which provide a realistic and fascinating insight into a bygone era of barbaric prison life.

OPENING HOURS: 9am - 4pm every day except Christmas Day.

COORDINATES: 32º14'45.36"S, 148º36'6.82"E

LOCATION: Off Macquarie Street in Dubbo.
