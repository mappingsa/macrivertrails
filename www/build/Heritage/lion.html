This classic country pub dates back to 1841 to the days of Cobb & Co. Today the Lion of Waterloo remains a charming tavern with modern food. It can also cater for functions and has a quiet beer garden, and in winter, an open fire near the bar to keep warm by as you enjoy the atmosphere.

OPENING HOURS: Open 4pm to close seven days. Meals from 6pm each day.

COORDINATES: 32º32'26.08"S, 148º55'50.49"E

LOCATION: Follow the signs off the highway...the tavern is on Montefiores Street-the turnoff is just near the highway bridge crossing the Macquarie river in the heart of Wellington.
