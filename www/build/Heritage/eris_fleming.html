This Gallery is uniquely situated in the large rambling gardens of a 130 year old farmhouse. It's 4 kms off the Mitchell Highway along a picturesque country lane. The Gallery houses a vibrant, colourful selection of Eris Fleming's oil paintings and bronze sculptures. These works have enduring appeal for both the collector and the investor.

OPENING HOURS: Friday, Saturday, Sunday, Monday 10am - 5pm. After hours by appointment

COORDINATES: 32º45'30.21"S, 148º55'41.44"E

LOCATION: From Wellington travel south 24 kms along the Molong-Orange road. Turn right at Woodside Lane and travel another 4 kms.
