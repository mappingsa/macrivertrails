Voted the Best Small Garden Centre NSW/ACT 2011 and 2012 and Accredited ‘Garden Centre of Excellence’. Edgerton Nursery is a complete garden centre specialising in plants suited to the harsh western NSW climate. Also featuring beautiful garden decor items, gifts and home-wares, garden furniture, pots and ornaments.

OPENING HOURS: Seven days. Monday - Friday 9am to 5pm. Saturday - Sunday 9am to 4pm

COORDINATES: 32º13'48.17"S, 148º14'22.80"E

LOCATION: Right in the heart of the main street of Narromine, just up from the United Services Memorial Club.
