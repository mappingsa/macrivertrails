A wonderful place to enjoy the peaceful surrounds and sounds of alpacas and the tranquility of nature. There is also the fantastic coffee shop in an old train carriage plus shearing exhibitions, produce for sale and talks on the alpacas.

OPENING HOURS: Open for bookings: Monday and Tuesday (10am-4pm). Open: Wednesday to Saturday (9am-4pm). Sunday - all day breakfast special included.

COORDINATES: 32º15'10.59"S, 148º15'51.77"E

LOCATION: “Nyrang” Dappo Rd, Narromine NSW (Off the Parkes Road. and 2 minutes past the Showground).
