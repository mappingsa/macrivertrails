Ewe Two on Dandaloo is never one to follow the crowd and this ensures the products available in the store are unique. This fantastic little emporium sells quality women's clothing as well as new lines of homewares. Ewe Two has become a drop-in centre for locals and a must-see destination for travellers and tourists. As well as fashion and homewares, Ewe Two also stocks up on local produce.

OPENING HOURS: Mon 9am-5pm, Sat 9am-1pm, Sun 11am-4pm. You can also shop online.

COORDINATES: 32º2'0.23"S, 147º59'8.86"E

LOCATION: On the sweeping bend of the Mitchell highway through Trangie.
