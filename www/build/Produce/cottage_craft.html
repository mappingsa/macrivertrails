Narromine Cottage Craft is a craft emporium selling high quality crafts, cooking, gifts and home-wares that are locally made or sourced. All goods are handmade in the local area by local residents, who form part of a co-operative with the aim of supporting local charities in the area while giving members the opportunity to express their creative talents.

OPENING HOURS: Monday-Friday 9.30am-5.00pm, Saturday: 9.00am-12.30pm, Sunday: 9.00am-12.30pm

COORDINATES: 32º13'50.71"S, 148º14'24.15"E

LOCATION: 61 Dandaloo Street - the main street of Narromine - on the opposite side to Coles, the Services Club and Edgerton Nursery.
