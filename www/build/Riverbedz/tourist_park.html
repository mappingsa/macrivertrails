This facility offers both classic powered and unpowered caravan and tent sites plus a motel. The motel has a large self-catering kitchen, dining room and TV lounge room. Located within the Narromine aerodrome precinct.

COORDINATES: 32º13'24.59"S, 148º13'47.75"E

LOCATION: Narromine Aerodrome on the Mitchell Highway, Narromine. Take the road to Nyngan and look for the aerodrome entrance on the edge of town.
