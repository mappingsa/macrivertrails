This 3-star river-front motel has all ground floor, air conditioned units, a pool, beautiful shaded lawns to relax, go fishing, or launch a canoe. This is also a good place to start the Macquarie River canoe and kayak trail (see separate description). Platypus watching is another attraction at this location.

COORDINATES: 32º32'39.57"S, 148º56'28.93"E

LOCATION: On the Mitchell Highway, next to the bridge over the Macquarie River at 5 Lee Street.
